A beautiful and professional way to showcase your services or products on your website

== Description ==

Our Services Showcase allows you to easily and professionally display your services, products, portfolio items or any type of list. Your site
visitors will see beautiful and animated list of your services in one of many templates. This plugin includes over 600 icons with 2 different icon-templates, also 
uses featured images.


Available Customizations:

* 2 featured image templates
* 2 icon templates
* includes over 600 icons dedicated for site owners
* Customize the number of columns
* Display social icons, phone number, email address and downloadable file
* Unlimited colors
* Add/edit/search/display your services
* Boosts SEO with schema.org markup
* Easy to use, no code knowledge required

Pro Version Features:

* 3 additional templates ( Carousel, Enhanced Grid & Accordion )
* 2 Single view templates ( lightbox card popup & Side panel )
* Click to call
* Circular or square images
* Flat or circular social icons
* Attributes section with percentage progress bars
